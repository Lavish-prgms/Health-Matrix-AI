# ⌚ Health-Matrix AI

Health-Matrix AI is an AI-based Smartwatch Health Monitoring System designed especially for rural healthcare environments. The system collects health data such as heart-rate, oxygen level, and body temperature, analyzes abnormal patterns, and sends early alerts to doctors.

---

## 🚀 Features

- Smartwatch based health monitoring
- Real-time patient monitoring dashboard
- AI-based abnormal detection
- Doctor alert system
- Patient priority ranking
- Rural healthcare focused solution
- Live health graphs
- Multiple patient monitoring

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

In many rural areas, people do not have access to continuous health monitoring and timely medical support. Basic health parameters such as heart-rate, oxygen level and body temperature are not regularly observed. Due to lack of early detection, health risks increase and treatment gets delayed.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

Health-Matrix AI collects health data from smartwatches and analyzes it using AI. If abnormal conditions are detected, alerts are generated and assigned to available doctors. The system displays all patient health status on a simple web dashboard.
